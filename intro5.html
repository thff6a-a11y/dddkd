Participating countries participate in Expo by establishing a special pavilion that displays their culture, innovations, and products. Expo is an important event in the world of culture for everyone and attracts the attention of audiences and global media. Expo is a huge event that lasts for several months, and is organized by the host country in cooperation with the BIE.
