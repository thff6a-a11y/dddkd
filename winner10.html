Many of the innovations that changed human lives were the product of World Expos, where every 5 years the World Expo comes, bringing with it creativity and innovations, including:

  1. Paris Expo 1855: washing machines

  2. London Expo 1862: The first mechanical computers

  3. Expo Philadelphia 1876: the first telephon

  4. Expo Philadelphia 1876: Red Tomato Sauce "Ketchup Heinz"

  5. Paris Expo 1889: Gramphone

  6. Expo Paris 1900: talking films are the nucleus of cinematic films

  7. Expo New York 1939: Television Set

  8. Expo Seattle 1962: IBM's first computer

  9. Expo Japan 1970: Mobile Phones
