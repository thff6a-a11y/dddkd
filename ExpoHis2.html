The first exhibition was held in London, United Kingdom in 1851, and was known as the "Universal Exposition" or "Crystal Exhibition". Since then, many exhibitions have been held around the world. The date of Expo changes from one session to another, as the dates of each session are determined based on the decision of the host country, the obligations related to the organization, and the necessary preparations. A time period of between three and six months is usually set for the exhibition. For reference, Expo Dubai 2020 was scheduled to be held in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, during the period from October 2020 to April 2021, but it was postponed due to the Covid-19 pandemic, and new dates were set for the period from October 2021 to October 2021. March 2022. To know more details about future Expo dates and host countries, it is recommended to visit the website of the International Federation of Expositions (BIE), which acts as the regulatory body for Expo and provides accurate information about upcoming events.
